About This Project

Step into a three-dimensional space dedicated to one of the most influential artists of all time.

This interactive gallery offers you the chance to explore some of Van Gogh’s most iconic works in a unique way — pieces that reflect his emotional intensity, vibrant color palette, and profound artistic vision. Enjoy a visually captivating experience, discover the stories behind each painting, and let yourself be inspired by Van Gogh's genius.

We wish you a pleasant exploration!

Controls

W/A/S/D: Move around

Mouse: Look around

Space: Toggle pointer lock

M: Show Menu

Enter: Start exploration

Esc: Stop exploration

G: Start audio guide

P: Stop audio guide
